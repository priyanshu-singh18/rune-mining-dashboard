  • INTRODUCTION
  • NODEAPES is an ordinals collection of apes living in nodes, rewards hodler runes on bitcoin and are powered by CBRC-20 metaprotocol.

  • LORE
  • An enigmatic journey has begun, 10,000 apes has entered nodes, successfully cracking the code of a mammoth Runic Enigma through their unwavering unity, unlocking access to powerful Runic Miners made with a purpose to secure runes for their community, which will be etched when rune protocol launches on Bitcoin. Let the halving games begin.

  • ECOSYSTEM
  • ART COLLECTION: pfp ordinals with programmed traits.
  • RUNIC ENIGMA: mines runes for hodler on bitcoin using runic miners.
  • ECONOMY: powered by CBRC-20 metaprotocol.

  • RULES
  • 1. Each NODEAPE inscription comes with a RUNIC MINER and CBRC-20 tokens to participate in the economy.
  • 2. Runic miner is automatically activated at a blockheight of 833,000 and starts mining runes.
  • 3. Runes will be allocated to an address with runic miner per bitcoin block until supply is fully allocated.
  • 4. Runes will be allocated based on NORMAL mode paired with RARITY, JUGGERNAUT and HALVENING modes of operation.
  • 5. Accumulated runes will be distributed once runes protocol launches on bitcoin.

  • ALLOCATION OF RUNES:
  • NORMAL: a fixed reward of 10 runes per block per runic miner.
  • +RARITY: rarer the ape, more runes a runic miner can mine per block. Rarity rank range of ape = #[1-100] = 20 | #[101-1000] = 10 | #[1001-2000] = 9 ... #[9001-10000] = 1 rune.
  • +JUGGERNAUT: a boosted reward of 10 more runes per block after ape drinks juggernaut which will be activated automatically at blockheight 834,000 and requires maintaining a balance of 100 CYBD (CBRC-20) tokens per ape per miner to keep boosting.
  • +HALVENING: at blockheight of 840,000, 100 random apes holding atleast 1 runic miner gets 10% of runes supply allocated.

  • TOKENOMICS
  • RUNE TOKENS: 10,000,000,000 [allocation is variable and happens once per bitcoin block]
  • CBRC-20 TOKENS: 10,000,000 [allocation is fixed and happens once per NODEAPE]

  • RUNES PROTOCOL INTRODUCTION
  • Ordinals creator Casey Rodarmor proposed the new protocol in September 2023, pitching it as a better and simpler alternative to the experimental BRC-20 standard for fungible tokens. Runes are fungible tokens issued directly on Bitcoin using the Runes protocol.

  • CBRC-20 Metaprotocol INTRODUCTION
  • Cy[bord] CBRC-20 is an efficient Metaprotocol standard on Bitcoin Ordinals that allows merging of image and tokens in one single inscription to ensure provenance.
